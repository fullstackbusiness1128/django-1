Member FAQ

Getting Started

Why use Leader Terminal?

You can find more career opportunities with ease compared to other executive-level job searching platforms.

As a member of Leader Terminal you will have access to many high-level job opportunities that haven’t been shown publicly.

Most of these positions you wouldn’t have heard about in the first place through traditional job searching platforms. Check out some of our member’s success stories.

What positions can I find on Leader Terminal?

We review everything submitted by employers, recruiters, and members to ensure that they are a good fit for you.

Most of these positions are Vice President level and above.

You can also customize the jobs you receive in your weekly email from Leader Theory by updating your email preferences here.

How much is this going to cost me?

You can Join Leader Terminal absolutely free.

As a Standard Member you will have the ability to see all Leader Terminal job listings.

If you upgrade to our “Full Access” membership, you’ll be able to see all of the hiring and contact information for each manager and recruiter for every job listing.

We also give you points for contributions which you can use to drop the cost of the “Full Access” membership.
Leader Terminal also provides space for companies and recruiters to post their jobs on the network.

Can I find jobs locally?

Of course you can!

Leader Terminal is a global community with roots in New York City, San Francisco, Los Angeles, Boston, Chicago, and other major cities in the United States as well as Canada.

When should I sign up?

You can join Leader Terminal whenever you like.

Our members use Leader Terminal to get a feel for the job market, share these jobs with peers, and do competitive benchmarking. There are plenty of opportunities out there for you and we provide the resources to get you where you need to go.

What types of companies do you work with?

We have a diverse group of companies you can choose from ranging from early-stage startups to established multinational corporations.

When we receive a job listing, we thoroughly vet the company and the role offered to make sure you’re getting the highest quality career opportunities possible.

Do you have any membership criteria?

We have high standards for our members and so we evaluate each applicant on their merit, career accomplishments, and overall progression.

We have a two-part evaluation process that assesses an applicant’s eligibility for membership, which includes an automated review of the applicant, combined with a peer review process (see peer review guidelines).

We don’t use a detailed rubric for vetting applicants because it limits our ability to assess the individual in a thoughtful and inclusive manner.

Reaching Out to Employers

What happens once I’ve redeemed a job posting?

After redeeming a job’s contact information, we recommend finding that contact’s LinkedIn profile to see if you have any common connections.

Getting introduced by a mutual connection leads to the highest response rates.

How many jobs can I redeem?

As much as you want!

Feel free to apply for all sorts of different positions if you feel that you are a good fit for the role.

If you upgrade to our “Full Access” membership, you’ll be able to see all of the hiring and contact information for each manager and recruiter for every job listing.

Am I obligated to go to the interview If I change my mind about a company?

No, if you decide that you no longer want to work with a company you will not have to continue with the interview phase and it will have no affect on your standing with us or the platform.

Your Leader Terminal Account

How do I set my email preferences?

In the “Email Preferences” section of your Manage Settings page you can:

  • Tailor your weekly jobs email to be more relevant to you by choosing the job functions and geographical areas that interest you the most.
  • Change the primary address where you’d like to receive Leader Terminal emails by selecting “Make Primary”.
  • Add email accounts or remove any that might be inactive.

Feel free to apply for all sorts of different positions if you feel that you are a good fit for the role. If you upgrade to our "Full Access" membership, you’ll be able to see all of the hiring and contact information for each manager and recruiter for every job listing.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password on your Manage Settings page under the “Profile Preferences” section. The email confirmation will be sent to your primary email address, which is also noted as “primary” on your Manage Settings page. If you can’t remember your old password (don’t worry, it happens all the time) you can:

  • Log out of your account and click the “Log In” button in the upper right-hand corner of Leader Terminal.com.
  • After entering your email, the next screen will ask you to enter your password. Click the link above the field that says “Forgot password?”.
  • Then check your primary email associated with your account. Within a few minutes you should receive an email containing a link to reset your password.

If you would like further assistance, please contact us at support@leadertheory.com

How do I unsubscribe?

If you would like to unsubscribe from the weekly job listings email you can do so by going to the Email Preferences section of your Manage Settings page and uncheck the box that says “I would like to receive Leader Terminal emails”.

How do I earn and use Leader Terminal points?
We have created a simple “give-to-get” point system in which members earn redeemable credit that they can trade-in to lower the cost of their “Full Access” membership. Each point you earn is worth the equivalent of $1.00 off the regular price. There are three primary ways to earn more Leader Terminal points (for future trade-in purposes):
  1. Refer new members and earn up to 2 points per referral. Each time you refer a new member to Leader Terminal you’ll earn 2 points. You can invite as many peers as you’d like on the Invite Peers page. As a bonus, we’ll also award you with an extra 1 point the first time you refer a peer who goes on to become an Leader Terminal member. You can check the status of your invites at any time in your Point History.
  2. Forward exec-level job opportunities to us and earn up to 6 points per submission If there’s an executive level job that you have been offered, but don’t intend to take please forward it to support@leadertheory.com. To receive maximum points, check out the job criteria here. All submissions are kept completely anonymous; and you’ll receive an additional bonus point for your first job contribution.

  3. Review applicants and earn up to 7 points per week Peer Review gives members the ability to contribute to the Leader Terminal applicant vetting process. For every 2 applicants you review you’ll receive one (1) point, and up to a maximum of seven (7) points per week.

Questions about Peer Review
Peer Review gives members the ability to contribute to the Leader Terminal applicant vetting process. For every 2 applicants you review you’ll receive one (1) point, and up to a maximum of seven (7) points per week.

Additional questions about reviewing peers:

Does my vote determine whether or not an applicant will be admitted to Leader Terminal?
In order to make membership decisions fair, we combine member perspectives on applicants alongside our internal automated review process in order to come up with a balanced and unbiased decision.

What are the criteria for entry?
We don’t use a detailed rubric for vetting applicants because it limits our ability to assess the individual in a thoughtful and fair way. When you have applicants to review you will see some guidelines of what we tend to look for: career progression, achievements, future potential, etc.

Why didn’t I see candidates this week?
In order to quickly approve applicants, we let members vet on a first-come-first serve basis. Given that there are more voters than applicants this means that on some days you won’t see peers to review.

Questions about Submitting a Job
If there’s an executive level job that you have been offered, but don’t intend to take please forward it to support@leadertheory.com. To receive maximum points, check out the job criteria here. All submissions are kept completely anonymous; and you’ll receive an additional bonus point for your first job contribution.

How are Leader Terminal's listings vetted?
Our operational team reviews all job submissions to ensure that they’re of the quality that’s expected of our high-caliber members. If you redeem a point for a particular job and you find out shortly after we posted it that the position has been filled, please contact us to let us know.

Do I need to do any vetting before I submit job opportunities to Leader Terminal?
No, just submit job opportunities for which you’re approached by executive recruiters (but aren’t interested in pursuing) to support@leadertheory.com and we’ll do the vetting for you.

Can I submit job opportunities from my employer?
Yes! We welcome all Director-level and above jobs submitted on behalf of your employer. If you’d like to see candidates for a job that you’re hiring, you can post your job here.

Your Privacy Leader Terminal Account

Will people know that I use Leader Terminal?

No, not unless you communicate it with your peers. All Leader Terminal member profiles are anonymous.

Can anyone see if I redeem a point on a job?

No, employers will only see your information if you choose to reach out to them.

Will anyone see that I’m the contributor of a crowdsourced job?

No, all contributions you make are kept confidential.
If you’re interested in engaging with Leader Terminal members, please reach out to us at support@leadertheory.com.
